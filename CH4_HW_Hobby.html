Planet Eclipse Etha 2

This is a Planet Eclipse Etha 2. I have owned this gun for a year but have only used it a handfull of times. This specific model is the "Pal Loader". A Pal Loader basically means there is a small mechanisms that allows air to come into the hopper and "jumble" the balls around to prevent sticking. It's a cheaper alternative to buying an electric hopper.























Empire Air Tank

This is the Empire Air Tank. It is one of the best and most used air tanks in paintball. Several players can be seen with this air tank on the field. It is due to its light weight and high air capacity.
